RHIZOM
KÜNSTLERISCHES GESTALTEN
MASTERPROJECT / SEMINAR /
Study of growth

The sculpture emerged from its center in a complex process while grewing in various directions over the course of the seminar.
It changes and varies its materiality, composition and orientation in stages of the manufacturing process.
The shape is therefore never planned and neither the starting point nor the end point of the sculpture can be defined.
At the same time, the sculpture is used to experimentally learn the different manufacturing processes for plaster of paris in differentiated mixtures with colored pigments.
Other materials used are bone glue and paper mache.
The sculpture is the result of the collaboration between Leon Landry and Irida Xanthou. Photographs by Paul Schusseil.
